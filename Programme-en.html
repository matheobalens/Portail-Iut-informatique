the Program

In this section, you will find the academic program that will be proposed to you in the Computer Science section for the next 6 semesters that you will find here.

The program offered in the IUTs allows students to prepare for working life, for this purpose the work proposed by all the teachers. is based on the student's autonomy while remaining at their disposal.

Learning in the DUT Informatique is based on 3 axes that require specific skills.

Analysis and Conception

The purpose of this section is to train students to maintain a relationship with the client to specify needs and expectations while conceptualizing a schema to process the information.

Algorithmic and Programming

Creation and processing of a coding by the different languages seen during the courses. (html/css, Python, JavaScript, PHP, C++ etc...)

System and network architecture

This part is used to discover the functioning of the computer and its operating system as well as networks such as Internet, intranet, OS...
