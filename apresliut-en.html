What to do after the DUT?

Continuing Studies

After obtaining the technological university degree, continuing studies is always possible: this is what 80% of students choose to do after their DUT.

If they make this choice, it is because young graduates have a large number of possibilities:

  • Professional licenses: An additional year to specialize in a specific field with the aim of to become operational in the labor market. This 3rd year takes place in the IUT, a high school or a university. It also offers experience in a professional environment as well as a specialization in specific trades.

  • University cursus in faculties: Computer training at the university allows students coming from a DUT in computer science to continue up to BAC +5, because this is what is most in demand in business, and thus obtain a basic license. Contrary to the professional license, this course allows to continue until the master.

  • Engineering schools: If you have not entered an engineering school after the baccalaureate, but becoming a computer engineer is now your project, after a 2-year IT DUT, this 3-year training allows you to deepen your knowledge, with the key to a bac + 5 level training in science and project management.

Work oportunities after the DUT

On leaving the DUT, students are not obliged to continue their studies. Indeed their DUT can offer outlets as assistant engineer or project manager in management and industrial IT. Immediately operational in software and hardware development, participation in the design, production and implementation computer systems according to the specifications submitted to them.

They have the capacity to meet the needs of companies in terms of network administration, design and implementation of programs, they can provide technical assistance, or be responsible for database management ...
