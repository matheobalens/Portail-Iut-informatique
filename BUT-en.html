To the B.U.T.

Presentation of the section to the B.U.T.

The University Bachelor of Technology (B.U.T.) is the new diploma of reference of the IUT.

By integrating a B.U.T., students benefit from an integrated course in 3 years, without additional selection to reach the degree license.

Degree is aligned with international standards to find employment in foreign countries

With the B.U.T., the UTIs reinforce their strengths: students continue to benefit from university and technological education supervised by experienced mixed educational teams.

The curriculum is always structured around work situations, periods in company in the form of internships (3 months) or alternating (sometimes from the first year or more generally over the last two years).

What IUT ?

IUT is...

  • a multitude of specialty choices

  • Selective training that is accessible to general and technological containers.

  • Adapted pedagogy with a large focus on directed and practical work in small groups

  • Recognized training and skills sought by companies

  • Educational teams involved and close to students

  • An academic curriculum that meets a national program

  • To join the IUT, you must register at the end of the year on the website Parcoursup

  • I get a new DUT after the first two years

  • Training is highly structured around work situations (600 hours of projects, 22-26 weeks of internship)

  • I can take part-time training on all or part of the course

  • Je peux bénéficier d'une mobilité internationale (voyage)

  • I am entitled to a university registration fee or exemption if I am a scholarship holder thanks to the CROUS
